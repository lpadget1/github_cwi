About

Lisa Padgett, Owner/Designer

In addition to the creativity that you expect from a graphic designer, Lisa has a unique skill set from a former career in civil engineering. She balances imagination with organization, while adhering to the budget, schedule and scope. She enjoy working with organizations and individuals who value integrity, organization, open communication and positive thinking—and, of course, animals.
