DCD is a community calendar for graphic designers in the Denver metro area.

Whether you are a student trying to figure out how to plug into the design community or a seasoned professional looking for fresh inspiration, DCD is here to help you quickly and easily find graphic design related events.

We list only events related to graphic design, since there are already a number of art and culture calendars.

We rely on DCD readers just like you to help keep the list current. “Take some info and leave some info” is our motto.

Ongoing Exhibits

Through Sunday, June 1, 2014

Rebranded: Polish Film Posters for the American Western

Denver Art Museum

Shane, The Misfits, Tom Horn, and Oklahoma! The DAM shows how these movies and other iconic westerns were reinterpreted by Polish artists in this exhibit.

http://www.denverartmuseum.org/exhibitions/rebranded-polish-film-posters-american-western-0



Sunday, March 2 – Sunday, June 8, 2014

Modern Masters: 20th Century Icons from the Albright-Knox Art Gallery

Denver Art Museum

Presented by Denver Art Museum and the Clyfford Still Museum, this exhibition brings together about 70 iconic artworks including van Gogh, Picasso, O’Keeffe, Dalí, Kahlo, and Warhol as well as one of Pollock’s finest drip paintings. In all, more than 40 influential artists will be on view.

http://www.denverartmuseum.org/exhibitions/modern-masterworks-albright-knox-art-gallery
