DCD is a community calendar for graphic designers in the Denver metro area.

Whether you are a student trying to figure out how to plug into the design community or a seasoned professional looking for fresh inspiration, DCD is here to help you quickly and easily find graphic design related events.

We list only events related to graphic design, since there are already a number of art and culture calendars.

We rely on DCD readers just like you to help keep the list current. “Take some info and leave some info” is our motto.

Why is it important for graphic designers to connect?

There are numerous art and cultural event calendars, but this community site narrows the topic down to GRAPHIC DESIGN.

Students

If you are a graphic design student, you need to know how to plug in to the local design community. Attending and reporting on design-related events may even be part of your school’s curriculum.

Freelance Designers

When you are working solo most of the time, you need to take a break to network and recharge your creativity. When you find an open spot in your calendar (or you are good about scheduling creative breaks), check this site for opportunities to sharpen your skills or just enjoy some creative conversation.

Design Firms

Reward your hard-working creative staff with an afternoon off to attend an interesting class or an evening out with a ticket to a fun event. Breaking away from the daily routine allows designers to recharge, plus they will also bring some fresh and cutting-edge ideas back to the office.
